PopMaster Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-23

Data Collection

This app does not require an account and does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. Game progress, settings, shapes, and theme preferences remain on your device and are not collected by the developer.

Purchases

The Pro unlock is sold through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes purchase and payment information. The app reads App Store transaction entitlement only to unlock Pro features.

App-Specific Notes

  • The app does not request camera, photo, location, microphone, or contacts access.
  • Sound and haptic feedback are user-controllable in Settings.
  • Two-player Duel runs on one device and does not create online player accounts.
