Holdr Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-23

Data Collection

This app does not require an account and does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. Holdings, transaction costs, saved wallet addresses, alerts, and preferences remain on your device and are not collected by the developer.

Purchases

The Pro unlock is sold through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes purchase and payment information. The app reads App Store transaction entitlement only to unlock Pro features.

App-Specific Notes

  • The app requests public market data from CoinGecko and the Fear and Greed Index from alternative.me. Requests may include ordinary network information such as an IP address and are subject to each source's privacy policy.
  • CSV contents and scanned wallet addresses are processed on device and are not uploaded by this app.
  • Concentration and technical insights are computed entirely on device; holdings and costs are not sent to a third-party AI service.
  • The app provides information and tracking tools, not investment advice or trading services.
