ScanFlow PDF Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-23

Data Collection

This app does not require an account and does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. Scans, OCR text, and PDFs are processed on device and stored in the app's local document space; the developer does not upload or read your documents.

Purchases

The Pro unlock is sold through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes purchase and payment information. The app reads App Store transaction entitlement only to unlock Pro features.

App-Specific Notes

  • Camera and photo access are used only when you choose to scan or import images.
  • The file picker grants access only to PDFs you select; the app does not browse other files.
  • A document leaves the app only when you intentionally share it, save it to Files, or send it to another app.
