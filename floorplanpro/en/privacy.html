FloorPlan Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-17

Data Collection

This app does not require account registration and does not use third-party advertising SDKs. App data is designed to stay on the device unless the user explicitly exports or shares it.

Purchases

The Pro unlock is sold through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes purchase and payment information. The app reads App Store transaction entitlement only to unlock Pro features.

App-Specific Notes

  • Floor plan projects are designed for local storage.
  • Export or sharing only happens when the user initiates it.
