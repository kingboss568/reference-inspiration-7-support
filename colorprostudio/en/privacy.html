ColorPro Studio Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-23

Data Collection

This app does not require an account and does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. Photo sampling, color conversion, palettes, and saved swatches are processed on device; the developer does not upload or collect your photos or color data.

Purchases

The Pro unlock is sold through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes purchase and payment information. The app reads App Store transaction entitlement only to unlock Pro features.

App-Specific Notes

  • Camera and photo access are used only when you choose to sample a color.
  • Palette data leaves the app only when you intentionally open the system share sheet.
  • The app does not claim medical, scientific, or calibration-grade precision.
